These spicy baked shrimp are made with a perfect blend of spices! So easy and quick to make!

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Additional Time: 1 hr
  • Total Time: 1 hrs 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 pound uncooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • cooking spray

Directions

  • Step 1: Whisk olive oil, Cajun seasoning, lemon juice, parsley, honey, soy sauce, and cayenne pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl.
  • Step 2: Add shrimp and toss to evenly coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  • Step 3: Turn the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Spray a baking dish with cooking spray.
  • Step 4: Transfer shrimp into the prepared baking dish and pour any remaining marinade over top.
  • Bake in the preheated oven until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, about 10 minutes.
